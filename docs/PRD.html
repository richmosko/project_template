1. Overview & Problem Statement

Why we're building this. The user problem, who feels it, and the desired business outcome.

Replace this block during the Research phase. Use /generate-prd to be interviewed.

1.1 High-Level Approach

The rough shape of how we'll tackle the problem — a sentence or two so a reader can squint and see the same solution. Not a design or architecture; just the framing. (e.g. for "feature discoverability" → "an in-app notification center for relevant new features".)

One paragraph max. Detail belongs in ARCH.html.

2. Goals & Success Metrics

What measurable outcomes prove the product succeeded? Mix measurable (metrics) and immeasurable (feelings). Include counter-metrics where relevant. Ideally in priority order.

Quantify success at 30 days and 90 days post-launch where possible.
  1. Goal — metric / target — counter-metric (if any)

3. Non-Goals

Explicitly out of scope — and why. Non-goals are as clarifying as goals: they shut down ambiguity, prevent scope creep, and make trade-offs visible.

  1. What we're not doing — and the reason (deferred / out of charter / different team / etc.)

4. User Stories & Use Cases
